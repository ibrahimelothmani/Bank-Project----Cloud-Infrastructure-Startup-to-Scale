Question 1
Is it recommended to put all functions inside the same service? Explain.

Answer:
No, it is not recommended. Putting all functions in one service leads to a large, complex class that is harder to maintain and test.
Separating services by responsibility  makes the code cleaner, easier to manage, and promotes better separation of concerns.

Question 2
In this design, setRoom(..) does not impact previous bookings. What is another way? What is your recommendation?

Answer:
Another way is to update room details only if no active bookings exist for that room, preventing inconsistencies.
Alternatively, use versioning or snapshotting: keep old room data for existing bookings and update the new room data separately.
I recommend preserving historical booking data unchanged to maintain booking accuracy and integrity.
